The **My Work** section of Zenn OS includes two reference areas that do not directly involve logging work but are essential for delivery: the **Project Vault** for managing creative assets, and the **Legal & IP** screen for reviewing the agreements that govern your engagement with the agency.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Project Vault">

## Project Vault

Navigate to **Project Vault** at `/team/vault` to browse, upload, and manage all assets linked to your assigned projects. The vault is organized by project and then by folder, giving you a structured place to store raw footage, exports, audio, project files, and client review packages.

### Browsing assets

The vault opens with a folder sidebar on the left and a file grid or list view on the right. Select a project from the tabs at the top, then choose a folder to see its contents:

| Folder | Typical contents |
|--------|-----------------|
| `01_Raw_Footage` | Unedited camera and screen recordings |
| `02_Audio` | Voice-overs, sound design, and music tracks |
| `03_Project_Files` | Editing project files (Premiere, After Effects, Figma, etc.) |
| `04_Exports` | Rendered outputs and final deliverables |
| `05_Client_Review` | Packages prepared for client feedback rounds |

Use the search bar to filter files by name within the active folder.

### Asset visibility

Every asset has a `visibility` value that controls who can see it:

- **`internal`** — visible only to agency team members. Clients cannot access these files through the client portal.
- **`client_visible`** — visible to the assigned client through their **Asset Vault** in the client portal.

When you upload an asset, its default visibility is `internal`. An admin or lead PM changes visibility to `client_visible` when a deliverable is ready to share. Check the asset's visibility before sharing a direct link to avoid exposing internal work-in-progress files to a client.

### Uploading assets

Click **Upload Asset** to open the upload modal. Drag a file onto the drop zone or click to browse, then set the file name, version string (e.g. `v2`), and destination folder. Click **Upload** to save. For a faster path when folder and version details are already set, click **Quick Upload** to add files directly without filling out the form.

You can also drag files directly onto the main file area; a drop indicator appears and files are versioned automatically.

### Versioning

Every asset maintains a `versions` array. Each entry records a `version` string, an `uploadedAt` timestamp, and a `url`. When you upload a new version of an existing file, a new entry is added to that array rather than overwriting the previous one. To download or reference a specific version, open the file's context menu (the three-dot icon) and choose **Download** or **Copy Link** — links are version-specific.

The `proofVersion` field on an asset identifies which version was formally submitted as proof for a milestone. Reviewers and admins can always retrieve the exact file that was submitted, regardless of how many newer versions you upload afterward.

## Legal & IP

Navigate to **Legal & IP** at `/team/legal` to view all contracts and agreements that have been assigned to you. Files appear here once an admin sends them through the contracts module.

### Contract types

As a team member, you are most likely to encounter the following contract types:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `contractor` | Contractor agreement setting out your engagement terms, rate, and scope of work with the agency. |
| `nda` | Non-disclosure agreement restricting you from sharing client or agency information outside the project. |
| `ip_transfer` | IP transfer or assignment agreement transferring ownership of work you produce to the agency or end client. |

You may also see a `sow` (statement of work) attached to a specific project, or an `msa` (master services agreement) if your engagement covers multiple projects.

### Contract status

Each contract in your list shows a `status` value so you know exactly where it stands:

| Status | What it means for you |
|--------|-----------------------|
| `draft` | The contract has been prepared but not yet sent to you. |
| `sent` | The contract has been sent to your email or is ready for review in the portal. |
| `viewed` | The agency can see that you have opened the document. |
| `partially_signed` | One or more parties have signed but the contract is not yet fully executed. |
| `executed` | All parties have signed. The agreement is fully in force. |

### Viewing your contracts

Select any contract card to open the document in the preview pane. Contracts relevant to a specific project include a `projectName` reference so you can match them to your active work on the assigned board.

<Note>
  You can only view contracts where you are the `recipientId`. Contracts assigned to other team members are not visible from your Legal & IP screen. Contact an admin if you believe a contract has been sent to the wrong recipient.
</Note>
