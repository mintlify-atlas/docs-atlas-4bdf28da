The **My Projects** board is the home screen for employees and affiliates in Zenn OS. It surfaces every active project where you are listed as a team member, giving you a single place to check status, review your milestones, and act on overdue items without navigating to the admin layer.

## Viewing your assigned projects

When you open **My Projects**, Zenn OS queries all projects where your user ID appears in the `assignedTeam` array and filters them to `status: active`. Each project card displays:

- **Project name** and client name
- **Project type** (e.g. `web`, `video`, `branding`)
- **Health status** — `green`, `amber`, or `red`, reflected as the card's accent color
- **Completion percentage** — a progress bar driven by the project's `completionPercent` value
- **Your milestones** — only the milestones where your user ID is in the milestone's `assignedTo` array are shown inline

From any project card you can click **Submit Proof** on an `in_progress` milestone to jump directly to the Proof Submission screen, or click **Vault** to open that project's asset vault.

<Note>
  The board only displays projects where your user ID is included in the project's `assignedTeam` array. If you expect to see a project but it is missing, ask your project manager to confirm your assignment.
</Note>

## Action required panel

The sidebar on the right of the board highlights items that need your immediate attention, such as milestones that have gone more than five days without a progress update. Click **Update Now** in any action card to navigate directly to the **Milestone Update** screen.

## My Calendar

The **My Calendar** view at `/team/calendar` renders a personal timeline scoped exclusively to your work. Milestone start dates and end dates from every project in your `assignedTeam` appear as deadline markers. Scheduled meetings and internal events relevant to your projects are also surfaced here.

Use the calendar alongside the board to plan your week — a milestone's `endDate` in the calendar corresponds to the same field in the `Milestone` object, so dates always stay in sync with the project data.

## Filtering and sorting projects

On the assigned board you can narrow and reorder your project list using the filter controls above the card grid:

- **Filter by status** — choose `all`, `on_track`, `at_risk`, or `overdue` to isolate projects by health signal
- **Sort by** — order by project name, completion percentage, or nearest deadline
- **Search** — type a project or client name to instantly filter the visible cards

Filters only apply within projects already scoped to your assignments; you cannot use them to view projects outside your `assignedTeam`.
