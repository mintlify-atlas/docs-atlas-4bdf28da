Two sections of the Client Portal handle the practical side of your engagement: **Billing** shows every financial transaction tied to your projects, and **Asset Vault** is where you download your approved final deliverables. Both are accessible from the sidebar under the **Client Portal** group.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Billing">

## Your transaction history

The Billing page lists all transactions associated with your account. From your perspective as a client, transactions with `type: "incoming"` represent payments you have made to the agency. Each row in the table shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `date` | When the transaction was recorded |
| `description` | A plain-language summary of what the payment covers |
| `amount` | The transaction amount |
| `status` | Current payment status (see below) |
| `invoiceId` | The invoice reference number, if one has been issued |

### Transaction statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `paid` | Payment has been received and reconciled |
| `pending` | Invoice has been sent and payment is expected |
| `overdue` | The payment due date has passed without receipt |
| `cancelled` | The transaction was voided and no payment is owed |

### Downloading invoices

Any transaction that has an `invoiceId` has a downloadable invoice. Click the download icon on the right side of any transaction row to save the invoice as a PDF.

<Warning>
  If any transaction shows a status of `overdue`, your project work may be paused until the balance is cleared. Contact your account manager immediately at the email address on the invoice, or reach out through **Chat Hub** in the sidebar.
</Warning>

## Downloading your deliverables

The Asset Vault is your library of approved final files. Only assets where `visibility: "client_visible"` are shown here — internal working files, raw footage, and unfinished drafts are not included in your view.

### Downloading a file

Click the download icon next to any asset to get the latest version. By default, Zenn OS always downloads the most recent entry in the `versions` array — you do not need to select a version manually unless you want an older one.

### Asset types

Your vault can contain any of the following file types:

| Type | Examples |
|---|---|
| `video` | Final cuts, social edits, brand films |
| `image` | Approved photography, graphics, renders |
| `document` | Final copy, reports, strategy documents |
| `audio` | Voiceovers, music beds, audio branding |
| `archive` | Packaged design files, font kits, full brand bundles |
| `design` | Editable source files, brand guidelines |

### Version history

Every asset in the vault has a `versions` array. Each version entry includes a label, upload timestamp, and direct download URL. To access an older version, expand the asset row and select the version you need from the history list.

<Note>
  Assets appear in your vault only after the associated milestone has been approved in Proof Review and the agency team has set the file to `client_visible`. If you are expecting a file and it is not showing up, check whether the corresponding proof has been approved, or ask your project manager through **Chat Hub**.
</Note>
