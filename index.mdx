## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in with your email">
    Navigate to the Zenn OS login page. Enter your agency email address — your role is detected automatically based on your email domain. No password complexity is required in this phase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    First-time users are routed through the onboarding flow before reaching their dashboard. Complete your profile to unlock full access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Admins land on the Executive Dashboard. Team members open their Assigned Board. Clients arrive at the Project Hub. From there, every feature of the platform is one click away in the sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Zenn OS uses email-based role detection. Your role determines which sections of the platform you can access. Contact your agency admin if you need to change your role or access level.
</Note>
