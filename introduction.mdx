Zenn OS is the operating system for creative agencies. It brings together every moving part of an agency — project pipelines, financial tracking, team coordination, client communication, and legal documents — into a single role-aware platform. What each person sees and can do is determined entirely by their role, so admins have full command over the business while team members, affiliates, and clients each work within a focused, relevant workspace.

## Key features

Zenn OS is built around the full lifecycle of an agency engagement — from first lead to final delivery and payment.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Executive Dashboard" icon="chart-line" href="/admin/executive-dashboard">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Financial Ledger" icon="book-open" href="/admin/finance">
    Track every incoming and outgoing transaction, reconcile invoices, flag anomalies, and maintain a clear financial record across all client engagements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payout Allocator" icon="credit-card" href="/admin/finance">
    Distribute earnings across team members and affiliates using fixed amounts or percentage-based splits, tied directly to project milestones.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Project management" icon="folder-open" href="/admin/projects">
    Manage the full project lifecycle with milestone tracking, dependency mapping, health status indicators, and an assigned team per engagement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CRM Inbox" icon="inbox" href="/admin/crm-intake">
    Capture inbound leads, move them through pipeline stages (from new inquiry to converted), score them by temperature, and flag spam automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Client Portal" icon="monitor" href="/portal/project-hub">
    Clients get their own dedicated space to view project milestones, submit approvals on proofs, download finalized assets, and review invoices.

## How Zenn OS is organized

The platform is divided into four access layers, each scoped to a user role.

| Layer | Role | Landing page |
|---|---|---|
| Command | Admin | `/admin/executive-dashboard` |
| My Work | Employee, Affiliate | `/team/assigned-board` |
| Client Portal | Client | `/portal/client-dashboard` |
| Platform | All roles | `/chat`, `/settings` |

<Note>
  Your role is detected automatically when you log in using your email address. Contact your agency admin if you need your role or access level adjusted.
</Note>
