---
title: "Get started: log in and reach your dashboard"
description: "Log in to Zenn OS with your agency email, complete one-time onboarding if needed, and reach your role-specific dashboard in minutes."
---

Zenn OS uses email-based role detection to route you directly to the right workspace as soon as you authenticate. There is no manual role selection — your access level is determined by your email address. Follow the steps below to go from the login page to your first dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Navigate to the Zenn OS login page at `/login`. You will see the **Enter Ecosystem** form with fields for your email address and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type your agency-assigned email address into the **Email Address** field, then move focus away from the field (click elsewhere or press Tab).

    Zenn OS detects your role from your email address as soon as you leave the field. A confirmation banner appears immediately below the email input:

    - **Administrator identity recognized** — your email maps to the `admin` role and elevated access is active.
    - **Client portal access confirmed** — your email maps to the `client` role.
    - **Team member identity confirmed** — your email maps to `employee` or `affiliate`.

    <Info>
      Role detection happens client-side based on the email-to-role mapping configured for your agency. If no banner appears, your email may not be registered. Use **Request Access** at the bottom of the login card to contact your admin.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Enter your password and click **Enter Ecosystem**. Zenn OS authenticates your credentials and checks whether your account has completed onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding (first-time users only)">
    If this is your first login, you are redirected to `/onboard` before reaching your dashboard. The onboarding screen displays your name, role, and an **Initialize Workspace** button.

    Click **Initialize Workspace** to confirm your identity and unlock full platform access. You are then routed to your role-specific dashboard automatically.

    <Note>
      The onboarding gate runs once per account. After your workspace is initialized, subsequent logins go directly to your dashboard — you will not see the onboarding screen again.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrive at your role-specific dashboard">
    After authentication (and onboarding if applicable), Zenn OS redirects you based on your role:

    | Role | Redirect destination |
    |---|---|
    | `admin` | `/admin/executive-dashboard` |
    | `employee` | `/team/assigned-board` |
    | `affiliate` | `/team/assigned-board` |
    | `client` | `/portal/client-dashboard` |

    Each destination is the primary workspace for that role. From here you have full access to every section available to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate using the sidebar">
    The left sidebar is your primary navigation. It shows only the sections relevant to your role — no hidden or greyed-out items. Use it to move between your dashboard, projects, finance tools, chat, and settings.

    <Tip>
      Click the small arrow button on the right edge of the sidebar to collapse it to icon-only mode. This gives you more horizontal space when working in dense views like the Financial Ledger or Dependency Architect. Hover over any icon while collapsed to see its label.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where each role lands

After a successful login, each role enters a different part of the platform:

- **Admin** — The **Executive Dashboard** at `/admin/executive-dashboard` shows agency-wide KPIs, P&L, project health, and the live activity feed.
- **Employee** — The **My Projects** board at `/team/assigned-board` lists every project and milestone currently assigned to you.
- **Affiliate** — Same as employee: `/team/assigned-board` shows your assigned work and submission queue.
- **Client** — The **Project Hub** at `/portal/client-dashboard` shows your active projects, milestone progress, and any proofs awaiting your approval.
